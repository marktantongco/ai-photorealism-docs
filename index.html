AI Photorealism Skills

A technical framework for achieving true photorealism in AI image generation through photographic physics, material science, and strategic prompt engineering.

01 — The Core Problem

The "AI look" — waxy skin, plastic textures, flattened lighting — is a user error, not a model limitation.

The Buzzword Trap

AvoidUse Instead
hyperrealisticSpecific lens specs
8K ultra-detailedSensor references
professional lightingNamed lighting patterns
beautiful skinAnatomical micro-details
Why Buzzwords Fail: "8K" and "hyperrealistic" tell the AI to average millions of mediocre stock photos, resulting in over-processed skin.

04 — Lighting Architecture

Core Patterns

Rembrandt

Key light 45° off-axis; triangle on shadow cheek. Dramatic, intellectual depth.

Butterfly

Centered above; butterfly shadow under nose. Beauty, glamour.

Loop

30–45° to side, slightly above. Flattering, approachable.

Rim/Edge

Behind subject. 3D separation, halo effect.

Natural Light

ConditionCharacteristics
Golden HourLow-angle warm; long soft shadows
OvercastSoft, diffused, low-contrast
Window LightDirectional; wrap-around
Blue HourCool, melancholic, cinematic

Advanced Terms

  • subsurface scattering — Light penetrates surface, scatters internally (critical for skin)
  • global illumination — Physically accurate light bounces
  • ray tracing — Accurate reflections, shadows

08 — Post-Processing

Consistency Workflow

  1. Seed Locking: --seed 12345 holds initial noise
  2. Character Reference: --cref face.jpg
  3. Style Reference: --sref style.jpg
  4. Inpainting: Fix specific errors surgically

Resolution Strategy

  • Generate at 4K+ native for detail
  • HiRes Fix: Upscale + add high-frequency detail
  • External AI: Topaz Photo AI, Lightroom
