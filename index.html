4. Lighting Architecture

Core Lighting Patterns

Rembrandt

Key light 45° off-axis; triangle on shadow cheek. Dramatic, intellectual.

Butterfly

Light centered above; butterfly shadow under nose. Beauty, glamour.

Loop

Light 30–45° to side, slightly above. Flattering, approachable.

Rim/Edge

Light behind subject. 3D separation, halo effect.

5. Skin Realism: Conquering Uncanny Valley

The Subsurface Scattering Imperative

In real skin: 6% direct reflectance, 94% subsurface scattering.

Micro-Texturing

  • visible skin pores, pore-level detail
  • vellus hair, fine peach fuzz
  • subtle blemishes, natural asymmetry
  • skin grain, natural skin variation
